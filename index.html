Traverse Computation

What is Traverse?

A traverse consists of a series of straight lines of known length related to one another by known angles between the lines. The points defining the ends of the traverse lines are called traverse stations. Traverse survey is a method of establishing control points, their positions being determined by measuring the distancs between the traverse station which serve as control points and the angle subtended at the various stations by their adjacent stations.
